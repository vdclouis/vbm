Verbeemen D.S. nv does not make use of cookies.  You can decline cookies by changing your internet browser settings so that no more cookies will be stored.  You can also use these browser settings to remove all information stored earlier.

Insight into data, amendment or removal:

You have the right to withdraw any consent you have given for data processing or to object to the processing of your personal data by  Verbeemen D.S. nv and have a right to data transferability.  This means that you can submit a request to us to forward the personal data we have about you in a computer file to you or to another organisation indicated by you.  You can send a request for insight, correction, deletion or data transfer of your personal data or a request to withdraw your consent or an objection to the processing of your personal data to info@verbeemen.be.

How we safeguard your personal details:

Verbeemen D.S. nv takes the protection of your data seriously and appropriate measures to counteract abuse, loss, unauthorised access, unwanted publication and unauthorised amendment are taken.  If you are under the impression that your data are not properly safeguarded or that there are indications of abuse, contact us via info@verbeemen.be.
